What's this?

Every child is special, with unique combinations of abilities and needs that affect learning. They
explore, question, and wonder, and by doing so, learn. Ludilala stimulates children’s curiosity.
In Ludilala, they explore animals, shapes, colours etc..

Language

It’s proven that the best time to learn a language is under the age of six. Ludilala combines animations with interactive activities. It offers an easy-to-use language-learning program in a fun for young learners. Children will learn words and phrases suitable for their age, needs, and interests.

Discovery

Babies are born learners, with a natural curiosity to figure out how the world works. Curiosity is the desire to learn. It is an eagerness to explore, discover and figure things out. Ludilala encourages children’s natural interest. They learn about time, seasons, weather…

Want to learn something new?

Children are confronted daily with rich opportunities to solve problems and exercise their own independent judgment. Each problem offers children a chance to exercise and build a foundation for critical thinking.
At Ludilala, activities such as puzzles, memory games, labyrinth etc. keep children’s mind active and help them understand how things work.

Math

At very early age, children got sense of quantity naturally.
At “Arithmetic”in Ludilala world, games, images and stories help children develop their numerical capacities and arithmetic skills.

Creativity

Children are full of imagination and creativity. At Creativity in Ludilala World, children’s creativity can be developed by colouring, drawing and handcrafts. In Ludilala world, children are encouraged to share their works of imagination to give others inspiration.

Music

Research has found that learning music facilitates learning other subjects and enhances skills that children inevitably use in other areas. “Music” at Ludilala offers an early childhood music development program. It stimulates their ears and eyes, benefits to their language development. At Ludilala, music is more than just music for children.

Reading

Reading books to children stimulates their imagination and expands their understanding of the world. It helps them develop many skills, such as language and listening, logical thinking, communication… In the reading world of Ludilala, children will find the knowledge that reading is fun. Their concentration and discipline get enhanced too.

About Ludilala

Protection

We are an innovative enterprise committed to the research and services of children's educational products. Our group has dedicated itself to children's educational products for more than a decade, yielding a wide variety of knowledge for children that incorporates teaching into playing. All our products are available all around the world.

Ludilala World’s group is comprised of a bunch of great illustrators, computer programmers, excellent designers of creative product ideas, and education experts. We have supported each other all the way to our destination, young parents full of bright ideas, cute girls and romantic guys, as well as experienced men that have focused on the education of children for more than a decade.
Ludilala World has always committed itself to providing children worldwide from 0 to 6 years of age with substantial knowledge that incorporates teaching into playing. It offers to them a wide variety of gamified digital learning contents, such as reading, counting, creativity, illustrated books, puzzles, interactive music etc.
It is like a magnetic field that attracts children to enter, and can also be manipulated by them to create and develop different ideas. Ludilala World hopes to be helpful to children to build their constructive and positive attitude to explore the world.
